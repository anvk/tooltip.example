JQueryUI Tools Tooltip Example:

Here are the steps to recreate a tooltip issue:

  • Hover your mouse cursor over the button to see that the tooltip says "before click!"
  • Click the button but do not move away the cursor. Tooltip should be changed to "after click!" but it is not
  • Move your mouse away from the button so that tooltip disappears and now hover it over the button again
  • You see that the tooltip text looks proper now
